I'm currently a Year 3 Undergraduate architecture student based in Singapore studying at The University of Melbourne. I have previously worked at Type0 Architecture after my National Service to gain some experience. I felt that the best way to learn about the industry was to get my hands dirty, being able to correspond with contractors, engineers and specialist was an eye-opening experience.

I discovered my love for design and architecture in secondary school when I took a subject called Design and Technology. This was where I got heavily stuck in the design process and grew to appreciate the beauty in creation. I pursued this passion by attending Sinagpore Polytechnics' Diploma in Architecture (DArch) programme. Following that I had to serve my National Service which was followed up by working at Type0 Architecture.
